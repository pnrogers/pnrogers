My name is Paige Rogers. I am originally from Bakersfield, California. I moved to Chicagoland about 3 years ago to pursue a degree in Health Communication and play soccer at Trinity Christian College. Being a student-athlete was challenging, but I learned a lot about time-management and multitasking.

I am excited to move on from being a student and enter the workforce full-time. My passion is helping people understand the complicated healthcare system. I am passionate about providing people with the information needed to make informed decisions about their well-being. I believe that knowledge is power, and that it is a basic human right to know about your health and to have the power to take part in the process.
